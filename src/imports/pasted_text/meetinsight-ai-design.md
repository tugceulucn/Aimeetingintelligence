Design a modern SaaS dashboard UI for an AI Meeting Intelligence platform called MeetInsight AI.

The product analyzes meetings and produces insights such as summaries, action items, decisions, and meeting productivity scores.

Target users are startup teams, product managers, remote teams, and sales teams.

The interface should feel similar to Notion, Linear, and Slack dashboards: clean, modern, minimal, and productivity-focused.

Use modern SaaS design patterns, rounded cards, soft shadows, and a calm professional color palette.

Primary color: deep blue or purple AI theme
Secondary: neutral gray
Accent: success green / warning orange for insights

Use dashboard layout with left sidebar navigation and top navigation bar.
